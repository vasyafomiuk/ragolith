# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and focus on the technical content of the discussion. Personal attacks, harassment, and sustained off-topic disruption are not acceptable.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that violates this code of conduct, email the maintainer at the address listed in [package.json](package.json). Reports are kept confidential.

The maintainer may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban participants whose behavior is incompatible with this code. The decision is at the maintainer's discretion and is not subject to appeal.
